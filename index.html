About Us

Fueling Good Vibes & Bold Flavors

Established in 2019 in the heart of Kandy, The Garage Cafe was born from a simple vision: to create the ultimate industrial-cool hangout spot. Inspired by classic garage aesthetics—blending raw metallic accents, cozy rustic lighting, and vintage automotive charm—our space is built for those who love high-energy vibes and great comfort food.

Whether you're looking to pit-stop for a quick coffee break, tune out the busy city noise, or rev up your engines over a feast with friends, our warm and laid-back space is designed for pure relaxation.

Behind our garage doors, the real magic happens in the kitchen. Driven by a passion for bold tastes, we specialize in heavy, honest comfort food packed with rich Sri Lankan spices that hit the spot every single time.
